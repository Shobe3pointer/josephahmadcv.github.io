About me

Hi, I am Shozab but everyone calls Joseph! Thanks for visiting my site, you have taken the first big step in looking for the right person for the tech role you've been wanting to fill. Previously, I had worked in financial data analytics and risk analysis, with my degree focusing in on these areas. However, as interesting as data analytics is, it didn't fuel my curiosity. From rabbit holes to dodgey data, data anlytics didn't seem right for me. Infact, I was more interested in the programming and query langauges being used. During my masters degree I gained even more exposure in coding through projects based on my degree, Complex Systems Modelling (I can promise you it's not as scary as it sounds). I spent my free time slowly learning the basics of SQL, Python and C++. The processes seemed so effortless, and once I understood one concept I tried to find ways to implement it elsewhere or to try more complex operations. Which brought me quite nicely to pursuing a career as a software developer. I see myself as being at the forefront of the tech world, wanting to stay ahead and keep up-to-date with the latest tech trends. Being a software developer, you never stop learning or discovering new concepts, and being the curious person I am, a career in software development will keep me asking questions and finding answers!
